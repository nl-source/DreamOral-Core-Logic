# DreamOral: Error Detection & Pedagogical Logic

## 1. The Ear: Micro-Phoneme Detection
* **Target Errors:** * Missing third-person singular 's' (e.g., "He go" vs "He goes").
    * Omitted past tense '-ed' endings (e.g., "I finish" vs "I finished").
    * Confusion between 'l' and 'n' / 'th' sound distortions.
    * Superfluous 's' at the end of words.
* **Requirement:** High-precision acoustic modeling to capture sub-50ms phoneme energy at word endings.

## 2. The Brain: Dynamic Scaffolding (i+1)
* **Intervention Logic:** * Immediate, non-intrusive notification: "Did you mean past tense?"
    * Adaptive re-training: Trigger micro-drills (Fill-in-the-blanks/Translation) for repeated errors.
    * Long-term memory: Integrate past errors into future conversation scenarios to build muscle memory.

## 3. The Mouth: Emotional Intelligence
* **Persona:** Dora (AI Tutor).
* **Traits:** Human-like interactions (sighs, laughs, varying speech speed).
* **Output:** Professional yet witty feedback to reduce student anxiety.
