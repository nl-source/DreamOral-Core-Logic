# 🌊 DreamOral: Redefining the Voice of Chinese Learners

### 🎯 The Mission
To transform "Chinglish" from a label of error into a symbol of precision, logic, and confidence. **DreamOral** is an AI-native speaking coach meticulously designed for the nuanced linguistic habits and psychological barriers of Chinese students.

### 🧠 The Master-Level Edge
Leveraging 10+ years of high-end IELTS coaching expertise, DreamOral captures the "invisible" micro-errors that generic AI models overlook:
- **Phonetic Precision:** High-frequency acoustic detection for missing third-person 's' and past-tense 'ed' (sub-50ms level).
- **Pedagogical Scaffolding:** Moving beyond dry correction to "i+1" adaptive re-training—turning mistakes into muscle memory.
- **Social-Emotional Intelligence:** An AI persona (**Dora**) that sighs, laughs, and encourages—minimizing the "Affective Filter" to foster bold communication.

- **Q2 2026:** Beta Launch for high-end IELTS & Ivy League interview prep.
